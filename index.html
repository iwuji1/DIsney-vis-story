Here we have clustered the disney movies into 9 broad categories. The size of the bubble indicates the inflationa adjusted revenue made by the movie. And the color shows the genre of the movie. Overtime as you move between time periods you can see how certain genres such as romantic comedy, action and thriller become more popular and develop over time. Disney really does a wide catelog of movies.

If scroll over the bubbles you can see more information about the actual movies. It should be pointed out that some data was taken out for the sake of the visulaisation. Any movies that did not have genre or a mpaa rating were removed from this dataset.

To be clearer about the comparision between genre, we can look at this graph. Here we can see perhaps unsurprisingly that musicals are the highest revenue generating movie type, with the first movie snow white being the highest grossing movie, inflation adjusted.
